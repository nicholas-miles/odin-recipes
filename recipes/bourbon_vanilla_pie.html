Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups oat milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 shots bourbon
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup flour

Steps

  1. Warm milk, cream, bourbon and vanilla over low heat until simmering
  2. Beat egg yolks with sugar and salt until smooth
  3. Whisk flour into egg yolks
  4. Temper egg mixture and whisk into warm milk
